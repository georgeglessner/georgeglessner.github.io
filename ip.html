Investment Philosophy

  • Invest in total stock market or S&P500 index funds.
  • Keep fees to a minimum. 0% is ideal!
  • Prioritize tax-advantaged accounts.
  • Don't forget to invest in yourself / family.
  • Don't try to time the market.
  • Be consistent.
  • Keep it simple.
  • Stay the course.
